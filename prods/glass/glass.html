Bongs/DabRigs

Bongs and dab rigs come in a variety of shapes and sizes, each designed to enhance your smoking or dabbing experience. For bongs, enjoy the stability of Beaker Bongs and the simplicity of Straight Tube Bongs, or experience advanced filtration with Recycler Bongs and Percolator Bongs. Unique designs like Round Base Bongs and powerful hits from Gravity Bongs offer diverse options. Artistic Ceramic Bongs add a touch of elegance, while Acrylic and Silicone Bongs provide durability and portability. Cool your smoke with Ice Bongs or choose Zong Bongs with their zigzag necks for extra cooling and style.

Dab rigs, on the other hand, are specially designed for concentrates, featuring compact designs for quick vaporization. Explore classic Beaker and Straight Tube Dab Rigs, or opt for Recycler Dab Rigs for continuous smoke filtration. Percolator Dab Rigs offer smooth, cool hits, while Mini Dab Rigs are perfect for on-the-go use. Silicone Dab Rigs are durable and easy to clean, and Glass Dab Rigs come in artistic designs for a visually appealing experience. Select the ideal bong or dab rig that suits your preferences and enjoy a smoother, more enjoyable session.

Bong/Dabrigs Percolators
Tree Percolator

Features multiple arms (often between 4 to 16) that filter smoke through slits or holes for a smooth hit.
Honeycomb Percolator

Contains multiple small holes that resemble a honeycomb, providing extensive diffusion and filtration.
Showerhead Percolator

Uses a disc-shaped percolator with several slits, distributing smoke evenly for a clean hit.
Matrix Percolator

A cylindrical percolator with multiple slits and holes for maximum diffusion and cooling.
Turbine Percolator

Creates a vortex effect by spinning the water and smoke, enhancing filtration and cooling.
Inline Percolator

A horizontal tube with multiple slits that diffuse smoke efficiently before it rises to the next chamber.
Fritted Disc Percolator

Uses a disc with a porous surface, offering excellent filtration through tiny holes.
Circ Percolator

A circular percolator with slits around its circumference, providing smooth diffusion.
Barrel Percolator

Resembles a barrel with multiple slits or holes, offering robust filtration and cooling.
Disc Percolator

A flat disc with slits or holes, providing excellent smoke diffusion and filtration.

Hand Pipes

Hand pipes are a classic and convenient way to enjoy your favorite herbs. They come in various styles, each offering a unique smoking experience. Choose from the timeless Spoon Pipes, compact One-Hitters, and stealthy Dugouts. For a smoother hit, try Bubblers, which combine the portability of a hand pipe with the filtration of a water pipe. Chillums offer a straightforward, no-frills option, while Sherlock Pipes add a touch of elegance with their curved stems. Explore Glass Blunts for a reusable alternative to traditional blunts and Steamrollers for powerful, direct hits. Discover the perfect hand pipe to match your style and preference.

Bubblers

Water bubblers offer a unique smoking experience by combining the portability of a hand pipe with the filtration benefits of a bong. They come in various styles, including Hammer Bubblers, which resemble a hammer and are easy to handle, and Sidecar Bubblers, featuring a side-mounted mouthpiece to prevent splashback. Double Bubblers have two chambers for extra filtration, while Concentrate Bubblers are designed specifically for use with waxes and oils. Additionally, Sherlock Bubblers add a touch of elegance with their curved stems, and Travel Bubblers are compact and durable for on-the-go use. Discover the perfect bubbler to enhance your smoking sessions with smooth, filtered hits.

Accessories

Glass accessories enhance your smoking experience by adding functionality and convenience to your bong setup. These accessories include Downstems, which help diffuse smoke for smoother hits, and Ash Catchers, which keep your bong cleaner by trapping debris. Percolators can be added to further filter smoke, while Glass Bowls and Slides offer customizable options for different smoking preferences. Other essential accessories include Carb Caps for controlling airflow, Dab Nails for concentrate use, and Glass Adapters to connect various components. Discover a range of glass accessories to elevate your smoking sessions with added performance and style.
