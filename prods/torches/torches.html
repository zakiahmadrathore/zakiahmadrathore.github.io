Incense/Incense Burner

Incense has been used for centuries across various cultures for its aromatic and therapeutic properties. It comes in various forms, with incense sticks and cones being among the most popular. When burned, incense releases fragrant smoke that can enhance meditation, relaxation, and spiritual practices. Incense burners are essential accessories that safely hold and support the incense while it burns, preventing mess and ensuring even burning.
